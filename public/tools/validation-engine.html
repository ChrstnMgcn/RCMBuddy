Validate Your Excel Data Against Custom Rules

Ensure the integrity and accuracy of your spreadsheets by setting up and running powerful validation rules.

About This Tool:

The **Validation Engine** performs comprehensive analysis for blank cells, "NULL" string entries, future dates, and duplicate rows. Additionally, you can apply **custom validation rules** by selecting a saved data dictionary. Selectively override issues from the overall Pass/Fail calculation. Generate a detailed summary report and export it with your data.

  • **Standard Checks:** Blank cells, "NULL" strings, Future Dates, Duplicate Rows.
  • **Custom Rules:** Apply rules from your saved data dictionaries (Required, Allowed Values, Numeric Range, Regex, Date Past, Unique).
  • **Override Issues:** Exclude specific column issues from the final data quality score.
